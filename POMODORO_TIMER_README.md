# Pomodoro Study Timer - Quick Reference

## Quick Start

### Accessing the Timer
1. Log in as a student
2. Navigate to **Pomodoro Timer** from the sidebar (marked with "NEW" badge)
3. Or visit: `/student/pomodoro`

## Main Features

### 🕐 Timer Tab
- **Large countdown display** with circular progress indicator
- **Start/Pause/Resume/Stop** controls
- **Subject selector** to categorize study time
- **Session counter** tracking your progress
- **Study tips** in the sidebar

### 📊 Analytics Tab
- **Total focus time** - Overall study duration
- **Session statistics** - Completion rate and counts
- **Study streak** - Current and longest streaks
- **Subject distribution** - Pie chart of time per subject
- **Productivity by hour** - Best study times
- **Focus time trend** - 30-day progress chart
- **Streak calendar** - GitHub-style activity heatmap

## Default Settings
- **Work duration**: 25 minutes
- **Short break**: 5 minutes
- **Long break**: 15 minutes
- **Long break after**: 4 sessions

## How to Use

### Starting a Study Session
1. (Optional) Select subject from dropdown
2. Click **Start** button
3. Focus on your studies until timer completes
4. Take a break when prompted

### Customizing Settings
1. Click the **Settings** icon (⚙️) in timer interface
2. Adjust durations to your preference
3. Toggle auto-start options
4. Enable/disable notifications
5. Click **Save Settings**

### Break Activities
When a work session completes:
- Modal appears with break suggestions
- Choose an activity:
  - 🧘 Stretch
  - 💧 Hydrate
  - 👀 Rest eyes
  - 🚶 Walk
  - 🧠 Meditate
  - 🍎 Snack

### Viewing Analytics
1. Switch to **Analytics** tab
2. View your study metrics:
   - Overall statistics
   - Subject breakdown
   - Productivity patterns
   - Consistency calendar
3. Use insights to optimize study habits

## Tips for Success

### 📚 Study Tips
- Eliminate distractions before starting
- Choose a single subject per session
- Follow the timer without interruption
- Take breaks seriously - they're essential
- Build a daily study streak

### ⏰ Break Tips
- Stand up and move around
- Stay hydrated (drink water)
- Rest your eyes (20-20-20 rule)
- Don't check social media
- Prepare for next session

### 📈 Maximize Productivity
- Study during your most productive hours (check analytics)
- Balance time across subjects
- Maintain your study streak
- Review analytics weekly
- Adjust settings to match your rhythm

## Keyboard Shortcuts
- **Space**: Start/Pause (when timer focused)
- **Esc**: Close modals
- **Tab**: Navigate between elements

## Notifications
- **Sound**: Plays when session completes (if enabled)
- **Browser**: Shows desktop notification (requires permission)
- **Visual**: Timer color changes per session type

## Color Guide
- 🔵 **Blue**: Work session (focus time)
- 🟢 **Green**: Short break
- 🔵 **Light Blue**: Long break

## Session States
- **Idle**: Ready to start
- **Running**: Timer counting down
- **Paused**: Timer stopped temporarily
- **Break**: Break period active

## Analytics Metrics Explained

### Focus Time
- Total minutes spent in work sessions
- Excludes break time
- Includes only completed sessions

### Completion Rate
- Percentage of sessions finished vs started
- Higher is better (shows discipline)
- Interrupted sessions lower this rate

### Study Streak
- Consecutive days with at least one session
- Resets if you skip a day
- Builds consistent study habits

### Subject Distribution
- Shows time spent per subject
- Helps balance your studies
- Identifies areas needing more focus

### Hourly Productivity
- Average focus time per hour of day
- Identifies your peak performance hours
- Schedule important subjects during peak times

### Streak Calendar
- Color intensity = study time:
  - Grey: No activity
  - Light green: <30 minutes
  - Medium green: 30-60 minutes
  - Dark green: 1-2 hours
  - Darkest green: 2+ hours

## Troubleshooting

### Timer not starting?
- Check if subject is selected (if required)
- Ensure timer is in idle state
- Try refreshing the page

### Notifications not working?
- Check browser notification permissions
- Enable notifications in settings
- Some browsers require HTTPS

### Analytics not showing?
- Complete at least one session first
- Check date range settings
- Ensure you're logged in as correct user

### Sound not playing?
- Check browser audio permissions
- Verify sound is enabled in settings
- Check device volume
