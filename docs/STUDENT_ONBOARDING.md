## 1. Welcome & Getting Started

### Your First Login

**Step 1: Access the Platform**
- Visit your school's platform URL (provided by your teacher)
- Or download the mobile app:
  - 📱 iOS: Search "EduPlatform" in App Store
  - 🤖 Android: Search "EduPlatform" in Play Store

**Step 2: Login**
- Enter your email address
- Enter your password (provided by school or sent via email)
- Click "Login"

**Step 3: Change Your Password**
- You'll be asked to change your password on first login
- Create a strong password:
  - At least 8 characters
  - Mix of letters, numbers, and symbols
  - Something you'll remember!
- Click "Update Password"

**Step 4: Complete Your Profile**
- Upload a profile picture
- Add your contact information
- Set your preferences
- Add parent/guardian contact (if not already added)

### Platform Tour

When you first login, you'll see a quick tour highlighting:
- 🏠 **Dashboard** - Your home page
- 📚 **Classes** - All your subjects
- 📝 **Assignments** - Homework and tasks
- 📊 **Exams** - Tests and results
- 📖 **Study Materials** - Notes and resources
- 🎯 **Goals** - Track your progress
- 🏆 **Achievements** - Your awards and badges
- 💬 **Messages** - Chat with teachers

Click "Next" to go through the tour, or "Skip" to explore on your own.

## 3. Viewing & Submitting Assignments

### Finding Your Assignments

**Navigate to:** Assignments

You'll see three tabs:
- **Active** - Current assignments
- **Completed** - Submitted assignments
- **Graded** - Assignments with grades

**Each Assignment Shows:**
- 📌 Subject and topic
- 👤 Assigned by (teacher name)
- 📅 Due date and time
- 🎯 Total marks
- ⏰ Time remaining
- 📎 Attachments

**Status Indicators:**
- 🟢 **Not Started** - You haven't begun yet
- 🟡 **In Progress** - You've started but not submitted
- ✅ **Submitted** - Awaiting grading
- ⭐ **Graded** - You received your grade

### Viewing Assignment Details

Click on any assignment to see:

**Instructions Tab:**
- Detailed description
- What you need to do
- Grading criteria
- Important notes

**Resources Tab:**
- Teacher's attachments
- Reference materials
- Related study materials
- Helpful links

**Submissions Tab:**
- Your submission status
- Upload area
- Previously submitted files
- Comments from teacher

### Submitting an Assignment

**Step-by-Step Guide:**

1. **Click on Assignment**
   - Review instructions carefully
   - Check due date
   - Note total marks

2. **Prepare Your Work**
   - Complete the assignment offline
   - Save in required format (PDF, DOCX, etc.)
   - Make sure file name is clear
   - Check file size (usually max 10MB)

3. **Upload Your Submission**
   - Click "Submit Assignment"
   - Drag and drop your file, OR
   - Click "Browse" to select file
   - You can upload multiple files

4. **Add Comments (Optional)**
   ```
   "I've completed all questions. 
   Question 5 was challenging - I've shown my working.
   Please let me know if you need any clarification."
   ```

5. **Review and Submit**
   - Check all files are attached
   - Review your comments
   - Click "Submit"
   - You'll see a confirmation message

6. **Confirmation**
   - You'll receive a confirmation email
   - Assignment moves to "Submitted" status
   - Teacher receives notification
   - You can view your submission anytime

### Late Submissions

**Before Deadline Passes:**
- Green "Submit" button
- Full marks available

**After Deadline:**
- Yellow "Submit Late" button
- May have mark deduction
- Check if late submissions are allowed
- Contact teacher if you have a valid reason

### Resubmitting Assignments

If allowed by your teacher:

1. Go to submitted assignment
2. Click "Resubmit"
3. Upload new files
4. Previous submission is saved
5. Teacher sees latest submission

**Note:** Some assignments don't allow resubmission - check carefully!

### Tips for Assignments

✅ **Do's:**
- Read instructions twice
- Start early, don't wait until last minute
- Save your work frequently
- Check file format requirements
- Review before submitting
- Keep a copy for yourself

❌ **Don'ts:**
- Submit without reviewing
- Upload wrong files
- Wait until last hour
- Submit incomplete work without explaining
- Plagiarize or copy
- Ignore teacher's feedback

## 4. Taking Quizzes & Exams

### Available Quizzes

**Navigate to:** Quizzes

**Types of Quizzes:**
- 📝 **Practice Quizzes** - No grades, just practice
- 📊 **Graded Quizzes** - Count toward your grade
- ⏱️ **Timed Quizzes** - Time limit applies
- 🔄 **Retake Allowed** - Multiple attempts possible

### Taking a Quiz

**Starting a Quiz:**

1. Click on quiz name
2. Read the instructions:
   - Number of questions
   - Time limit (if any)
   - Allowed attempts
   - Grading method

3. Click "Start Quiz"
4. Confirm you're ready (can't pause once started)

**During the Quiz:**

**Question Types You May See:**

1. **Multiple Choice**
   ```
   Q: What is 2 + 2?
   ○ A) 3
   ○ B) 4  ← Select this
   ○ C) 5
   ○ D) 6
   ```

2. **Multiple Select (Choose all that apply)**
   ```
   Q: Which are prime numbers?
   ☐ A) 2  ← Check this
   ☐ B) 3  ← Check this
   ☐ C) 4
   ☐ D) 5  ← Check this
   ```

3. **True/False**
   ```
   Q: Earth is the largest planet
   ○ True
   ○ False  ← Select this
   ```

4. **Short Answer**
   ```
   Q: What is the capital of France?
   [Paris]  ← Type your answer
   ```

5. **Essay/Long Answer**
   ```
   Q: Explain photosynthesis
   [Large text area for your answer]
   ```

**Quiz Interface:**

- Question counter: "Question 5 of 20"
- Timer (if applicable): "15:30 remaining"
- Navigation: "Previous" and "Next" buttons
- Mark for review: Flag questions to revisit
- Answer tracker: Shows answered/unanswered

**Navigation Tips:**
- Click "Next" to move forward
- Click "Previous" to go back
- Use question numbers to jump to specific question
- Questions marked for review show orange flag

**Submitting Your Quiz:**

1. Answer all questions (or as many as possible)
2. Review flagged questions
3. Check unanswered questions (highlighted in red)
4. Click "Submit Quiz"
5. Confirm submission (can't change after this!)
6. View results (if immediate feedback is enabled)

### Exam Mode

For formal exams:

**Before the Exam:**
- Check system requirements
- Test your device and internet
- Find a quiet place
- Have necessary materials ready
- Login 10 minutes early

**During the Exam:**
- No pausing allowed
- Auto-submit when time expires
- No going back once submitted
- Follow exam rules strictly
- Contact support immediately if technical issues

**After the Exam:**
- Results published as per teacher's schedule
- Review exam (if allowed)
- Check answer explanations
- Note areas for improvement

### Practice Mode

Use practice quizzes to prepare:

**Benefits:**
- Unlimited attempts
- Immediate feedback
- See correct answers
- Track your progress
- No pressure!

**Study Strategy:**
1. Take practice quiz
2. Review wrong answers
3. Study those topics
4. Retake quiz
5. Improve your score

1. Click "New Message"

3. Enter subject line
4. Write your message:

## FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

**Q: I forgot my password. What do I do?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on login page. Enter your email and follow the instructions sent to you.

**Q: Can I submit an assignment after the deadline?**
A: Check if your teacher allows late submissions. There might be a penalty. Always contact your teacher if you have a valid reason.

**Q: How do I know if teacher graded my assignment?**
A: You'll receive a notification. Check the Assignments → Graded tab.

**Q: Can I delete a submitted assignment?**
A: No, but if resubmission is allowed, you can upload a new version.

**Q: Why can't I see my quiz results immediately?**
A: Some quizzes show results immediately, others after the deadline. Teacher decides this setting.

**Q: How often should I check the platform?**
A: Daily! Check morning and evening for updates.

**Q: Can I use the platform on my phone?**
A: Yes! Download the mobile app or use mobile browser.

**Q: My internet is slow. Can I still use the platform?**
A: Yes. Download materials when you have good connection and use offline mode.

**Q: Who can see my grades?**
A: Only you, your teachers, and your parents. Your peers cannot see your grades.

**Q: How do I earn more badges?**
A: Stay consistent! Complete assignments on time, maintain attendance, help classmates, and achieve your goals.
