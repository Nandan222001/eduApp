# Subscription & Billing - Quick Start Guide

## Overview
This guide will help you quickly set up and use the subscription and billing management interface.

## Access the Feature

1. **Login** as an institution admin
2. **Navigate** to the admin dashboard
3. **Click** on "Subscription & Billing" in the left sidebar menu (💳 icon)

## Main Interface

The subscription page has **6 tabs**:

### 1. Change Plan
- View all available subscription plans
- Switch between billing cycles (Monthly/Quarterly/Yearly)
- Compare features across plans
- Upgrade or downgrade your subscription

### 2. Payment Methods
- Add new credit/debit cards
- Delete old payment methods
- Set a default payment method for auto-billing

### 3. Invoices
- View all invoices
- Download invoices as PDF
- Check payment status
- View billing periods and amounts

### 4. Add-ons
- Browse available add-on modules
- Enable/disable add-ons with toggle switches
- See pricing and features for each add-on

### 5. Settings
- Enable/disable auto-renewal
- Configure payment reminder emails
- View subscription summary

### 6. History
- View timeline of all subscription events
- See payment history
- Track plan changes

## Top Section (Always Visible)

### Current Subscription Card
- Shows your current plan name and status
- Displays billing cycle and price
- Shows days remaining until renewal
- Provides cancel/renew buttons

### Usage Tracking Card
- Shows student count vs. limit
- Shows total users vs. limit
- Shows storage used vs. limit
- Color-coded progress bars (green/orange/red)

## Common Tasks

### How to Upgrade Your Plan

1. Go to **Change Plan** tab
2. Select your preferred billing cycle (save with yearly!)
3. Click **Compare Features** to see detailed comparison (optional)
4. Find the plan you want
5. Click **Upgrade** button
6. Review the confirmation dialog
7. Click **Confirm**
8. ✅ Done! You'll be charged a prorated amount

### How to Add a Payment Method

1. Go to **Payment Methods** tab
2. Click **Add Payment Method** button
3. Fill in card details:
   - Card Number
   - Card Holder Name
   - Expiry Month/Year
   - CVV
4. Click **Add Card**
5. ✅ Card added! Set it as default if needed

### How to Download an Invoice

1. Go to **Invoices** tab
2. Find the invoice you need
3. Click the **Download** icon (⬇️)
4. ✅ PDF will be downloaded

### How to Enable an Add-on

1. Go to **Add-ons** tab
2. Find the add-on you want
3. Toggle the switch to **ON**
4. Review pricing in the confirmation dialog
5. Click **Confirm**
6. ✅ Add-on enabled! It will appear on your next invoice

### How to Configure Auto-Renewal

1. Go to **Settings** tab
2. Toggle **Enable Auto-Renewal** switch
3. Select when you want payment reminders:
   - 3, 7, 14, or 30 days before renewal
   - Or no reminder
4. Click **Save Settings**
5. ✅ Settings saved!

## Understanding Status Indicators

### Subscription Status
- 🟢 **Active** - Your subscription is active and in good standing
- 🔵 **Trialing** - You're in a trial period
- 🟡 **Grace Period** - Payment failed but you still have access
- 🔴 **Past Due** - Payment failed and needs attention
- 🔴 **Expired** - Subscription has ended
- ⚫ **Canceled** - Subscription was canceled

### Invoice Status
- 🟢 **Paid** - Invoice has been paid
- 🔵 **Open** - Invoice is pending payment
- 🔴 **Overdue** - Invoice payment is late
- ⚪ **Draft** - Invoice is being prepared

### Usage Status (Progress Bars)
- 🟢 **Green** - Normal usage (< 75%)
- 🟠 **Orange** - Medium usage (75-90%)
- 🔴 **Red** - High usage (> 90%) - Consider upgrading!

## Tips & Best Practices

### 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Save Money**: Choose yearly billing to save 20% compared to monthly
2. **Monitor Usage**: Check usage regularly to ensure you're on the right plan
3. **Set Reminders**: Enable payment reminders to avoid missed payments
4. **Default Card**: Always have a default payment method set
5. **Download Invoices**: Download invoices regularly for your records
6. **Try Add-ons**: Test add-ons during trial periods to see if they're worth it

### ⚠️ Important Notes

- **Upgrades** take effect immediately and you'll be charged a prorated amount
- **Downgrades** take effect at the end of the current billing cycle
- **Cancellations** allow access until the end of the current billing period
- **Auto-renewal** ensures uninterrupted service
- You need at least one payment method if auto-renewal is enabled

## Troubleshooting

### Q: Why can't I delete my payment method?
**A:** You can't delete your default payment method if it's the only one. Add another card first, set it as default, then delete the old one.

### Q: My usage is at 90%, what should I do?
**A:** Consider upgrading to a higher plan to avoid hitting limits. Go to "Change Plan" tab to upgrade.

### Q: How do I cancel my subscription?
**A:** Click the "Cancel Subscription" button on the overview card. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period.

### Q: When will I be charged for an add-on?
**A:** Add-ons are prorated and added to your next invoice. If you enable mid-cycle, you'll only pay for the remaining days.

### Q: Can I get a refund?
**A:** Contact support through the main menu for refund requests. Refund policies vary by plan.
